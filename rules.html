LuxCube Rules

Welcome to the official LuxCube rules! Please keep up-to-date with the rules as they may change from time to time!
To appeal a punishment create a ticket on our Discord.
Last Revised - 2/27/23 (MM/DD/YYYY)

NOTE - These rules are general guidelines, staff may impose additional punishments at their discretion.
The duration of the punishment will depend on the severity of the situation as well as past offenses.

Player Server Rules

- * Players are expected to adhere to LuxCube's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
- * Failure to comply with these rules may result in consequences, including but not limited to suspension or termination of your server.

Respect Other Players’ Servers

- Example: Do not destroy or "grief" other player-hosted LuxCube servers unless you own that server or have permission to do so.

No Cheating

- Example: Knowingly taking advantage of account bots on your server to boost the server higher up on the LuxCube server list is not allowed.

No Server Promotion

- Example: Using your server to advertise or move people away from LuxCube’s service is not allowed. It’s okay to advertise social media (Discord, Twitter, etc.), but you may not advertise IPs or links to servers hosted outside of LuxCube.

Keep it Clean

- Example: All server names, MOTDs, and content on the server must be appropriate. This includes any sort of racial slurs, discrimination, references to inappropriate or predatory content, as well as inappropriate or not safe for work content. Recreations of real-life events such as the Holocaust or school shootings, as well as anything else that might discriminate or spark hate against any cultures, sexual orientations, groups, or religions are not permitted.

Global Rules

- * These rules are enforced throughout all of LuxCube’s platforms, including Discord, Forums, Lobby, and player-owned LuxCube servers.

Mind Your Language

- Example: You are not allowed to swear or use curse words anywhere unless otherwise mentioned. Attempting to bypass our swear filter will result in a punishment.

No Threats or Inappropriate Content

- Example: Do not make threats against LuxCube, other players, or any websites and services. Posting any content that might be considered inappropriate, NSFW (not safe for work), or includes any references to drugs or alcohol is not allowed. This applies throughout all LuxCube platforms, including skins, usernames, images, statuses, posts, and anything else you are able to edit and change.

Respect Other Players

- Example: You may not post content that might potentially harm, trigger, or be sensitive to players. This includes content such as seizure-inducing images. Any sort of harassment, discrimination, or disrespect is strictly prohibited. This includes anything targeted against races, religions, gender/sexual orientation, as well as any other groups people might align with.

No External Advertisement

- Example: Any sort of external advertisement is not allowed. This includes any unsecured, suspicious, or malicious websites, as well as any scam/fraudulent websites. You also may not advertise any external Minecraft or Discord servers other than LuxCube or Super League’s official platforms.

No Spamming

- Example: You are not allowed to spam or “flood” any chats, channels, or servers. This includes usage of excessive capitalization. This also includes any attempts to use or act like a bot or “spambot” to mass spam messages or joins on any LuxCube platform.

No Personal Information

- Example: Asking or leaking another player’s personal or private information is strictly prohibited. This includes but is not limited to IP addresses, age, full names, house addresses, location, or anything else that might be considered private or self-identifying.
